Functional Requirements

The System is the central means of co-ordination between all the users of the SRC movement. It provides a platform of learning, discussion, innovation and execution to the various users like students, mentors, community-based participants and the companies/sponsors. The system is a website that must perform the following actions and interface with all the users

Students

Allow students to register to the courses, storing data like the school name and their area of specialisation (for Masters/PhD students). Perform authentication on every student who is returning to the system.

  • Student must be able to listen to Video lectures and read lecture notes.
  • Provide a quiz at the end of each lecture and store the individual student performance.
  • Create a general discussion platform to discuss questions across all users.
  • A separate link should be there in the home page to list all the available project descriptions and for the students to express their interest on a particular project.

Faculty

  • Allow a faculty to register with the system, with his school name, area of interest and his current research.
  • Display the problems that are posted by students/community people in a particular order that a faculty wishes to view. By default he must view the projects in the decreasing order based on the interest expressed by the sponsors. If he wishes he should also be able to view the projects based on his area of interest.

Community Participants

  • Allows the communities to register with the website
  • Allows the Community to post the problems, mentioning the seriousness of the problem and the date and time they need to get it solved.
  • Get the user notified when students /companies show interest in their problem

Companies

  • System allows the companies to go through all problems list that is posted by different communities
  • Can dive into the project description and details if they are interested in a particular problem and its proposed solution as described by the faculty.
  • Provide an easy interface for the companies to contact the faculty through the website, if the company is interested in knowing more about the project or wish to pursue it further.
  • Receive regular updates on the progress of the projects they are funding.

Administrators

  • Maintains the whole system.
  • Sending notifications to Students, Faculty, Mentors and Companies if a new problem is added.
  • Notifying the students/Mentors of the particular project if their project is being funded by a company.
  • Contact the company which showed interest in any of the projects by pressing the fund button. If they are new to the website administrator registers the company and will provide them with their user credentials

Other General Requirements for the website

  • Google custom search A google custom search will be implemented to search the main website and related blogs.
  • Live chat and messaging facility The website will have a chat interface and message sending facility to discuss with other users. The live chat will provide a quick communication. Messages will also be helpful for a formal communication between a mentor/faculty and a student.
  • Mobile Integration The webpages are required to properly fit on mobile devices and all the features will be supported across all major mobile operating system platforms.
  • Multiple lingual The website will be displayed in at least 5 different languages. With a button click on the main page, the language of the complete website will be changed.
  • Sharing The users can share a post of the website through social networking sites like Facebook, twitter. Sharing can also be done through sending mail to friends.
  • Auto-Response An automatic mail sender facility to notify the user for comments on their posts. Default auto mail response feature on registration.
  • Donate/Get Involved A page for the users who wish to donate to a particular project. This page will have link with PayPal for online money transaction.
