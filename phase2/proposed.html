Proposed Solutions

We believe that the most logical and desirable solution for this problem, from the domain prospective would be re-vamping the website (http://src.cse.buffalo.edu/). Though the existing site can serve the cause to some extent, there are several issues surrounding it, as discussed in the “Current Solutions and Their Deficiencies” section of this document. In order to make the website more usable, we will try to present a content driven design where the content (information) and user experience are placed up-front.
